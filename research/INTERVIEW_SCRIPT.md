# 10-question discovery (no pitch)

Use in DMs or short calls. Goal: learn how they work today — **not** to sell.

1. When you think about **last month’s sales or flips**, what’s the first place you look — spreadsheet, platform app, accountant, guess?  
2. Walk me through **the last time** you exported data. What tool, how long, what broke?  
3. What number do you **trust least** — gross, net after fees, profit after shipping, something else?  
4. If you use a **spreadsheet**, who built it — you, a template, someone you hired?  
5. What would make you **abandon** that spreadsheet? (missing feature, time, errors)  
6. For **fees and shipping**, do you know them before you list, after sale, or only at tax time?  
7. How do you decide **go / no-go** on a buy or listing price today? (rule of thumb, calculator, gut)  
8. What **software** do you already pay for in this stack? (even $5/mo counts)  
9. If you had **one extra hour** a week back, what would you stop doing?  
10. Who else should I talk to who has **the same headache** but a different setup?

**Close:** “Thanks — I’m exploring how people do this without a perfect tool. Can I follow up in a month with what I learned?”

**Do not:** demo your product, quote price, or ask “would you buy” until patterns repeat.
